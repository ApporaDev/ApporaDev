Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your information

Last updated: September 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PaperCloud, a Sri Lankan past papers application developed by Appora. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • • Account Information: Email address, name, and profile information when you create an account
  • • Authentication Data: Login credentials and authentication tokens
  • • Google Sign-In: Basic profile information if you choose to sign in with Google

2.2 Usage Information

  • • App Usage: Papers downloaded, subjects accessed, and app interactions
  • • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • • Download History: Record of papers downloaded for offline access

2.3 Technical Information

  • • Firebase Analytics: App performance and crash reports
  • • Push Notifications: Device tokens for sending notifications
  • • Local Storage: App preferences and offline content

3. How We Use Your Information

  • • Provide and maintain the PaperCloud service
  • • Authenticate users and maintain account security
  • • Track download history and provide personalized experience
  • • Send important app updates and notifications
  • • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

We use Firebase services provided by Google for data storage and processing:

  • • Firebase Authentication: Secure user authentication
  • • Cloud Firestore: User profiles and app data
  • • Firebase Storage: PDF files and app resources
  • • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • • With your explicit consent
  • • To comply with legal obligations
  • • To protect our rights and safety
  • • With service providers (Firebase/Google) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • • To protect the rights and safety of Appora and our users

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • • Access: View your personal information stored in the app
  • • Update: Modify your profile information
  • • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • • Opt-out: Disable push notifications in app settings

7. Children's Privacy

PaperCloud is designed for educational use and may be used by students under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your personal information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • • Email: apporadev@gmail.com
  • • App: PaperCloud - Sri Lankan Past Papers

Note: This app is designed to help Sri Lankan students access educational materials. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing the best possible educational experience.
